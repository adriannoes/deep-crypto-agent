### Features

* Uses multiple machine learning technologies to intelligently optimize strategies
* Guides strategies in live trading to improve real-world effectiveness and beat the market

### Supported Investment Markets:

* US stocks, A-shares, Hong Kong stocks
* Futures, Options
* Bitcoin, Litecoin

### Engineering Design Goals:

* Separate basic strategies from strategy optimization supervision modules
* Improve flexibility and adaptability

* Quantitative System

ABU Quantitative Comprehensive AI Big Data System, K-line Pattern System, Chan Theory, Elliott Wave Theory, Harmonic Theory, Breakout, Consolidation Pattern Analysis (Head and Shoulders Pattern, Triple Top, Triangle, Flag, Wedge, Rectangle),
Classic Indicator System, Trend Analysis System, Time Series Dimension System, Statistical Probability System, Traditional Moving Average System for deep quantitative analysis of investment products, completely crossing the complex code quantitative stage for users, more suitable for ordinary people to use, moving towards Quantitative 2.0 era.

* Quantitative Models

The above systems combine hundreds of sub-quantitative models, such as: Financial Time Series Loss Model, Deep Pattern Quality Assessment Model, Long-Short Pattern Combination Evaluation Model, Long Pattern Stop Loss Strategy Model, Short Pattern Cover Strategy Model, Big Data K-line Pattern Historical Combination Fitting Model, Trading Position Psychology Model, Dopamine Quantitative Model, Inertial Residual Resistance Support Model, Long-Short Interchange Revenge Probability Model, Strength-Weakness Confrontation Model, Trend Angle Change Rate Model, Linkage Analysis Model, Time Series Overreaction Model, Sluggish Revenge Reaction Model, Trend Initiation Speed Model, Pair Hedging Model, etc.

* AI Quantitative

ABU Quantitative develops algorithms from the bottom layer for AI artificial intelligence, building an artificial intelligence AI system suitable for quantitative systems, training several scoring models that identify quantitative features from different angles, overall divided into three categories: Physics Model Group, Dopamine Biology Model Group, Quantitative Pattern Model Group. Different model groups evaluate trends from different angles (mainly physical trading entity analysis, crowd psychology, charts and other three directions), model groups are composed of several unique recognition algorithms and parameter genetic elimination, forming populations, weighted voting scoring.

* Quantitative Strategies

ABU Quantitative combines traditional code-based quantitative systems, predicting the timing of future timing signals, the system is based on hundreds of simple seed trading strategies, deriving more quantitative trading strategies, new strategies continuously self-learn, self-grow, continuously split, survival of the fittest, reproduce under elimination selection mechanism, currently applied quantitative buy-sell signal strategies total 18,496 kinds.

* Quantitative Applications

ABU Quantitative combines multiple quantitative analysis data to build hundreds of quantitative applications,
such as: AI High Energy Warning, AI Highlight Moment, Intelligent Prediction of Rise and Fall, Downward Five Wave Quantitative, Upward Five Wave Quantitative, Chan Theory, Elliott Wave Theory, Harmonic Theory, Breakout, Consolidation Pattern Analysis (Head and Shoulders Pattern, Triple Top, Triangle, Flag, Wedge, Rectangle),
Resistance Support Strength Analysis, Ascending Triangle Breakout, Descending Triangle, Triple Bottom (Head and Shoulders Bottom), Triple Top (Head and Shoulders Top), Round Top, Round Bottom, Dark Cloud Cover Pattern, Rising Three Steps Pattern, Good Friend Counterattack Pattern, Single Needle Bottom Pattern, Shooting Star Pattern, Multiple Cannon Pattern, Rising Tweezers Line, Upward Box Breakout, Gap Breakout, Golden Ratio Line Quantitative, Trend Following Signal, Mean Reversion Signal, Stop Loss Risk Control Quantitative, Take Profit Protection Quantitative, Comprehensive Indicator Analysis, etc.
